Welcome to
LangLit

UGC Approved Journal - Arts and Humanities - Serial No. - 4921 & 8131

Langlit (ISSN 2349-5189) is an internation refered, indexed, Peer-Reviewed and open class journal published quaterly from latur, Maharashtra,India (August,November,February and May). it aims to publish original research articles and enrich research .All Contributions Submitted For publication in the Langlit Have been subjected to a rigorous review process by the editorial board and in-house editors. This journals uses double-blid review, Which means that both the review and authors identies are concealed from the reviewers, and vice versa, throughout the review process.

With Language, Literature, Education, Culture and Humanities., The basic purpose of the journal is to provide a publication platform for the talented young research scholars globally. to submit high-quality written works presenting original research with profound ideas and insightful thoughts. It was enlisted in the UGC Approved Journal – Arts and Humanities – Serial No. – 49124 & 8131 –
