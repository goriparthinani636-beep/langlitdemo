About Us

Dr.P.Pradeep’s Journal of
Multidisciplinary Research (DPPJMR)

International, refereed, indexed, peer-reviewed and open access journal published quarterly from NTR District, Andhra Pradesh, India (January, April, July and October). It aspires to publish unique research publications and foster a research culture among young researchers.

It also encourages imaginative and creative writers from all over the world to submit papers on science (all streams), engineering (all streams), language, literature, education, Buddhism, Jainism, Hinduism, Christianity, culture, and humanities.

Our Aim & Scope

The journal's primary goal is to provide a publication platform for brilliant young researchers from throughout the world. It aims to facilitate the sharing of ideas, perspectives, opinions, and counter-points among researchers. The editorial board invites scholars and writers to submit high-quality written works that exhibit original research, meaningful ideas, and perceptive perspectives. The Journal adopts the new UGC rules.

Our Journal Objective

The Journal's goal is to share creative views about global issues. This Journal will attempt to document new elements of science, engineering, religious studies, English language, and literature teaching that will benefit both teachers and students. Potential contributors are expected to use their research papers to promote constructive teaching and learning. We also included some whole new sections. We are honoured to publish and share the work of so many dedicated and insightful people around the world. Please visit the current issue page to read about the amazing writers and their perspectives on English Literature, Language, Criticism, Education, Humanities, and Culture. We hope that by reading these pieces, you will open up new avenues of conversation and appreciate the new adventure of some positive

LangLit blends with excellence. We nurture young learners in a setting that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and holistic development.
